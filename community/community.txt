-- Documentation of the suggestions system --

1) Using the command //suggest in the channel #suggestions-challenges will create an embed messsage
   with upvoats and downvotes reactions.
2) The goal is to allow users to share their opinion and suggestions with the community and the staff.

3) Using the command //challenge in the channel #suggestions-challenges will create an embed messsage
   with upvoats and downvotes reactions.
4) The goal is to allow users to share their ideas for improving JustAPuzwick's Yt Channel's content.
